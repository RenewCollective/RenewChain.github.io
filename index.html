About Renew³

Organization Overview:

Renew³ is a groundbreaking 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that transforms disaster response through innovative technology and community-centered coordination. We specialize in rapid home restoration services, connecting disaster-affected families with licensed contractors through our comprehensive mobile platform.

Our Vision: To become the leading nonprofit resource for post-disaster home restoration by creating a nationwide network of vetted contractors accessible through a user-friendly mobile application that prioritizes speed, reliability, and community care.

Core Values:

  • Rapid Response: Emergency services deployed within 2-6 hours of request
  • Community-First: Localized support with cultural sensitivity and personal care
  • Transparency: Open financial reporting and clear service documentation
  • Innovation: Technology-driven solutions for maximum efficiency
  • Integrity: Licensed, verified contractors with quality guarantees

Legal Structure & Compliance:

  • 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status with IRS
  • State registrations for charitable solicitation
  • GuideStar/Candid transparency certification
  • BBB Wise Giving Alliance standards compliance
  • FEMA and state emergency services partnerships

Target Beneficiaries:

  • Homeowners and renters impacted by natural disasters (hurricanes, floods, fires, tornados, earthquakes)
  • Low-income and uninsured individuals with limited recovery resources
  • Rural and underserved communities with limited contractor access
  • Elderly, disabled, and vulnerable populations requiring additional support
  • Communities affected by both sudden disasters and long-term environmental challenges

Service Areas & Coverage:

  • All 50 U.S. states with priority in disaster-prone regions
  • Focus on hurricane corridors (Gulf Coast, Atlantic Coast)
  • Wildfire zones (California, Pacific Northwest, Southwest)
  • Tornado Alley and severe storm regions
  • Flood-prone areas and coastal communities

Key Strategic Partnerships:

  • Government: FEMA, HUD, state emergency management agencies
  • Relief Organizations: American Red Cross, United Way, Salvation Army
  • Corporate Partners: Home Depot, Lowe's, local supply companies
  • Technology: Google.org, Microsoft Philanthropies for cloud infrastructure
  • Insurance: Major carriers for streamlined claims processing
  • Faith-Based: Local churches and religious organizations for community outreach

Innovation & Technology:

  • AI-powered contractor matching based on skills, location, and availability
  • Real-time GPS tracking and project management tools
  • Blockchain-based service verification and payment processing
  • Multi-language support and accessibility features
  • Integration with weather services and early warning systems

Impact Measurement:

  • Families served and homes restored
  • Average response time from request to service
  • Customer satisfaction scores and quality metrics
  • Community resilience improvement indicators
  • Cost savings compared to traditional recovery methods

Founding Story:

Renew³ was founded in response to the growing gap between disaster occurrence and effective recovery coordination. Recognizing that existing disaster relief often leaves families waiting weeks or months for essential home repairs, our founders developed a technology-first approach that treats rapid home restoration as a human right, not a luxury.

Leadership & Governance:

  • Board of Directors with disaster relief, technology, and nonprofit expertise
  • Advisory Council including emergency management professionals
  • Community liaison network for localized decision-making
  • Independent financial oversight and annual third-party audits

Emergency Home Repairs

Critical repairs to make your home safe and habitable after disaster strikes.

Services Include:

  • Roofing: Emergency tarping, leak repair, structural assessment
  • Structural Repair: Foundation stabilization, wall repair, window/door replacement
  • Water Damage: Extraction, drying, mold prevention
  • Storm Damage: Tree removal, debris clearing, temporary weatherproofing

Response Time: Emergency services typically deployed within 2-6 hours of request, depending on disaster scope and contractor availability.

Priority System: Life-threatening repairs are prioritized, followed by essential habitability repairs.

Utility Services:

  • Electrical Systems: Power restoration, panel repair, safety inspections
  • Plumbing: Water line repair, sewage system restoration, fixture replacement
  • HVAC: Heating and cooling system repair, ductwork restoration
